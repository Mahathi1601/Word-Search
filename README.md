# Word Search Game

🔗 Live Demo: https://mahathi1601.github.io/Word-Search/

![Game Screenshot](screenshots/gameplay.png)

Find hidden tech words in a letter grid. Click and drag to select words. Each correct word gives 10 points. Use points to get hints. Find all words to win.

**How to play**
Look at the word list. Find those words in the grid. Click and drag across letters to select. If correct, the word is marked found.

**Features**
New words every game | Score points | Get hints | Reset selection | New game

**Run locally**
Download index.html, style.css, script.js and open index.html in your browser.
